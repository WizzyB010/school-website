About Wonder Heights

Welcome to Wonder Heights

Wonder Heights International School extends a warm welcome to all prospective learners and families, inviting them to embark on a journey of education that transcends conventional boundaries.

Our institution is dedicated to fostering the individual talents of each learner, creating an environment conducive to academic, social, and emotional growth — grounded in Christian values of responsibility, discipline, and service.

Why Choose Wonder Heights

Where Values Meet
Academic Excellence

Our school is grounded in Christian ideals — responsibility, discipline, excellence, ethical behaviour, leadership, and service beyond self.

Early Drop-off & Late Pick-up

We operate from 7:30am to 5:00pm, accommodating working parents with flexible hours and before/after-school care.

Safe, Spacious Environment

Temperature-regulated classrooms, secure perimeter, and trained staff ensure every child is safe and comfortable all day.

Dedicated, Passionate Educators

Our teachers love what they do. Every school day is filled with exciting activities and personalised attention for each learner.

Library & Computer Laboratory

A well-stocked library and modern computer lab give students access to resources that expand their world beyond the classroom.
