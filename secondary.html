  JHS 1 – 3 · Ages 12 – 16
Programme Overview

Prepared for BECE & Beyond

Our Lower Secondary / JHS programme prepares students thoroughly for BECE success and placement in senior high school. We combine rigorous academic training with leadership, STEM focus, and career awareness — building the whole student, not just the exam candidate.

Full BECE Preparation

Past questions, mock exams, topic-by-topic revision, and targeted exam technique coaching for every subject.

Leadership & Prefect System

Students earn responsibility through school governance, prefect roles, and community service projects.

STEM Focus

Project-based learning in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics — including lab practicals and coding.

Career & SHS Counselling

Dedicated guidance counsellors help students choose the right SHS programme and plan their future career path.

  SHS 1 – 3 · Ages 16 – 19+
Programme Overview

Launching Ghana's Next Generation of Leaders

Our Upper Secondary programme offers a challenging, well-rounded education aligned with the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE). Students choose from Business, Science, General Arts, or Technical elective streams — each designed to open doors to Ghana's top universities and colleges abroad.

Full WASSCE Preparation

Comprehensive coverage of all WAEC syllabi, mock exams, marking scheme practice, and individual coaching.

Science, Business & Arts Streams

Students select their elective block in SHS 1, tailoring their studies to their university and career goals.

Tertiary Pathway Support

University application guidance, personal statement coaching, and scholarship awareness for top Ghana and international institutions.

Career-Readiness Programmes

Internship partnerships, industry visits, entrepreneurship workshops, and professional skills training.
