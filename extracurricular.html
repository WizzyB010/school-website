  All Grade Levels
Programme Overview

Nurturing Talents Beyond the Classroom

At Wonder Heights, we believe a truly well-rounded education extends beyond textbooks. Our vibrant extracurricular programmes offer students the chance to explore their passions, develop new skills, and build lifelong friendships in a supportive and creative environment.

Computing & Tech Innovations

From building modern websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to exploring digital design, students develop essential 21st-century IT skills.

Athletics & Sports Tournaments

Fostering teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness through competitive sports, including our highly anticipated school basketball tournaments.

Music & Creative Arts

Students can join the school choir, learn to play instruments like the keyboard, and participate in vibrant praise and worship sessions.

Media & Photography

A hands-on club for aspiring creators to learn camera settings, videography, and digital storytelling by capturing school events.

Explore Your Interests

Web Dev & Coding Club

Mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and problem-solving to build real-world digital projects and web applications.

Basketball & Athletics

Training, conditioning, and competing in local and regional inter-school sports tournaments.

School Band & Choir

Developing vocal talents and keyboard skills for assemblies, special school events, and cultural performances.

Photography & Media

Learning essential life skills and creativity by preparing simple, delicious meals like Indomie specials and hotteok.

Building confidence, critical thinking, and public speaking skills through structured debates and presentations.
