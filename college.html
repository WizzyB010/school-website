Programme Overview

Beyond Secondary — Into the Future

Wonder Heights College offers a rigorous two-year advanced programme for graduates and mature students who want to deepen their academic foundation, gain professional certifications, or strengthen their profile for entry into Ghana's top universities and international institutions.

Advanced Academic Certificates

International-standard advanced courses in Science, Business, Humanities, and Technology that are recognised by tertiary institutions.

University Pathway Support

Personalised university application coaching, personal statement guidance, and scholarship application support for Ghana and abroad.

Professional Skills & Internships

Structured internship placements, industry mentorship, CV writing, and interview preparation through our employer network.

Global Competency Programmes

Soft skills, leadership, communication, digital literacy, and cross-cultural competency — preparing students for a global workforce.

Academic Tracks

Choose Your College Pathway

Each track is delivered by specialist faculty and designed to align with university entry requirements, professional certifications, and real-world career demand.

Science & Technology

An advanced science track ideal for aspiring engineers, medical professionals, data scientists, and researchers. Students build deep competency in core sciences with extended laboratory work and technology integration.

Advanced Physics Advanced Chemistry Biology Elective Mathematics Computer Science Research Methods

Business & Economics

A professionally-oriented track for future entrepreneurs, economists, finance professionals, and business leaders. Combines rigorous academic content with applied business projects and entrepreneurship modules.

Advanced Accounting Business Management Economics Statistics Entrepreneurship Financial Literacy

Humanities & Social Sciences

An intellectually rich track for aspiring lawyers, journalists, policy makers, educators, and social scientists. Develops critical thinking, persuasive writing, and a deep understanding of society, governance, and culture.

Literature History Government & Politics Psychology Philosophy Media Studies

Digital Technology & Innovation

A cutting-edge track for students passionate about technology, design, and the digital economy. Covers coding, UI/UX design, data analysis, and digital entrepreneurship — aligned with the skills Ghana's growing tech sector demands.

Web Development Data Analysis UI/UX Design Networking Basics Digital Marketing Cybersecurity Intro

Cross-Track Enrichment Modules

Every college student, regardless of track, benefits from our enrichment modules designed to produce well-rounded, globally competitive graduates.

Public Speaking & Debate

Academic Writing

Critical Thinking & Logic

Your Journey

From College to Career or University

Our structured two-year pathway ensures every student leaves Wonder Heights College ready — academically, professionally, and personally.

1

Enrollment & Placement

Assessment, track selection, and personalised academic plan with your assigned advisor.

2

Year One — Foundation

Core track subjects, enrichment modules, and introductory professional skills training.

3

Internship & Projects

Industry internship placement, capstone research project, and mentor engagement in Term 3.

4

Year Two — Advanced

Advanced electives, university application preparation, and mock interviews with industry partners.

5

Graduation & Placement

Certificate award, university enrolment support, or career pathway activation through our alumni network.
