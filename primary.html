Programme Overview

Excellence Across Every Subject

Our Primary School offers a robust academic programme that seamlessly integrates the rigorous standards of the British Primary Curriculum with the cultural and civic requirements of the Ghana Education Service (GES). We develop critical thinkers, confident communicators, and young leaders who are ready for the global stage.

Dual-Curriculum Advantage

Mastery of core British subjects (English, Mathematics, Science) alongside essential GES learning areas.

ICT & Computer Literacy

Computer lab sessions from Year 1, building digital skills, typing proficiency, and safe internet habits.

Modern Foreign Languages

French instruction introduced from Year 4 — preparing students for a multilingual, globalised world.

Continuous Assessment

Termly exams plus ongoing formative assessment to track progress towards British Checkpoint readiness.

Core Subjects

What We Teach

English Language

British-aligned reading, writing, comprehension, grammar, and oral communication skills.

Mathematics

Arithmetic, geometry, measurement, and data handling with a focus on logical problem solving.

Science

Inquiry-based biology, chemistry, and physics concepts through hands-on experiments.

Our World and Our People

GES-aligned social studies, Ghanaian history, civics, culture, and citizenship education.

Computing (ICT)

Digital literacy, practical software skills, internet safety, and introductory coding.

French (Year 4–6)

Conversational French, basic grammar, and Francophone cultural awareness.

Religious & Moral Ed.

Christian values, ethical decision-making, and strong character formation.

Creative Arts

Visual arts, music, drama, and cultural studies for holistic expressive development.

Physical Education

Sports, fitness, teamwork, and instilling healthy lifestyle habits for life.
