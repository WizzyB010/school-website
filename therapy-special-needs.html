Programme Overview

Every Child Deserves to Thrive

Our Therapy & Special Needs programme is built on one unwavering belief: every child can learn, grow, and succeed when given the right environment and support. Our specialist team works alongside classroom teachers and families to provide compassionate, evidence-based intervention tailored to each child's unique profile.

Speech & Language Therapy

Supporting children with speech delays, articulation difficulties, fluency issues, and communication challenges.

Occupational Therapy

Developing fine motor skills, sensory integration, daily living skills, and classroom-based functional tasks.

Learning Support (Dyslexia & ADHD)

Individualised strategies, modified instruction, and specialised resources for diverse learning profiles.

Behavioural & Emotional Support

Positive behaviour frameworks, counselling, and resilience-building for children's mental wellbeing.

Areas of Support

Conditions & Needs We Support

Speech & Language Delays

Late talkers, articulation difficulties, stuttering, and expressive/receptive language disorders.

Dyslexia & Reading Difficulties

Phonological processing support, multisensory reading instruction, and literacy intervention.

ADHD & Attention Difficulties

Structured routines, behavioural strategies, and classroom accommodations for focus and engagement.

Autism Spectrum (ASD)

Social skills development, sensory support, visual schedules, and structured communication approaches.

Fine & Gross Motor Delays

Occupational and physiotherapy support for handwriting, coordination, and physical development.

Emotional & Behavioural Needs

Anxiety, social-emotional learning, coping strategies, and positive behavioural support plans.

Our Approach

How We Work With Your Child

Initial Assessment

A thorough evaluation by our specialist team to understand each child's unique strengths and needs, with no judgment and full confidentiality.

Individual Education Plan

A personalised IEP is developed for each child, with clear goals, timelines, and agreed strategies co-created with parents.

In-Class & Pull-Out Support

Therapy is delivered both inside the classroom alongside peers and in dedicated small-group or individual sessions.

Family Partnership

Regular parent meetings, home strategy guides, and open communication so families can reinforce progress at home.

Qualified Therapists

Certified speech, occupational, and behavioural specialists on staff full-time

Confidential Support

All assessments and records are kept strictly confidential — your trust matters
