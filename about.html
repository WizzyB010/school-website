Our Journey

School History

Wonder Heights International School was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide world-class education rooted in African values to children in Afienya and Greater Accra. Over the years, we have grown from a small community school into a respected institution recognised for academic excellence and character development.

Today, we serve hundreds of students across our Early Childhood, Primary, and Junior High programmes, with a dedicated team of educators who are passionate about transforming young lives. Our campus continues to grow with modern facilities including a computer laboratory, well-stocked library, and safe recreational spaces.

School Guidelines

Policies & Code of Conduct

Our policies ensure a safe, fair, and productive learning environment for all students and staff.

Admissions Policy

Wonder Heights admits students of any nationality, gender, or background. Enrollment is subject to availability of space and an assessment of the child's readiness for the programme applied for. Required documents include birth certificate, immunisation records, and passport-sized photographs.

Uniform & Dress Code

All students are required to wear the official school uniform at all times. The uniform must be clean, neat, and properly worn. Hair must be neat and natural. Excessive jewellery is not permitted. Details of the uniform can be obtained from the school office.

Attendance & Punctuality

  • School starts at 7:30am and closes at 3:30pm; extended care available until 5:00pm.
  • Students are expected to be punctual every school day.
  • Absences must be reported to the school office by 8:00am.

Code of Conduct & Discipline

Students are expected to show respect for all members of the school community, care for school property, and uphold the values of the school at all times. Bullying, dishonesty, and disrespect will not be tolerated. Disciplinary measures follow a clear progressive structure.

Phone & Electronics Policy

Mobile phones and personal electronic devices are not permitted on school grounds without explicit teacher permission. Devices will be confiscated and returned to parents if found in unauthorised use.

Health & Safety Policy

The safety and wellbeing of every student is our highest priority. All medications must be declared. Sick students will be isolated and parents notified immediately. The school maintains a first aid station and emergency protocols at all times.
